Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

How many years have you been in business? Are you licensed, insured, and bonded?
We have been in business for 23 years, fully licensed and insured to operate in this area, with bonding to protect your investment.
Can you provide references from previous clients or examples of homes you have built? Can I visit any completed projects?
Yes, we can provide contact details of past clients and arrange visits to homes we have constructed so you can assess the quality of our work.
What brands of materials do you use? Can you specify the quality and source of materials?
We use reputable brands such as SANKAR , DALMIA , and if substitutions are necessary, we will inform you and ensure the alternatives meet quality standards.
How long will the construction take? What are potential causes of delays?
The project is estimated to take 8 months. Delays can occur due to weather, supply chain issues, or unforeseen site conditions. We will keep you informed throughout.
What is the payment schedule? How many stages are there, and what percentage is due at each stage?
Payments are divided into stages such as deposit, foundation completion, framing, roofing, and final completion, with percentages allocated to each milestone.
Do you provide a warranty on the construction? What does it cover and for how long?
Yes, we offer a warranty covering structural defects and workmanship for 15 years. We will also address any warranty claims promptly.
Can I customize the floor plan or provide my own? What options and upgrades are available?
You can modify our standard plans or submit your own. We offer various options and upgrades to suit your preferences and budget.
Who is responsible for obtaining permits and ensuring zoning compliance?
We handle all necessary permits and ensure the project complies with local zoning and building codes.
Who will oversee the construction? Can I contact the site supervisor directly?
A dedicated construction coordinator will manage the project and keep you updated. You can contact the site supervisor for any questions.
Are your workers insured? What safety measures do you have in place?
All workers are insured and bonded. We follow strict health and safety protocols to ensure a safe working environment.
